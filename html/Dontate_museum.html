WHY DONATE TO MUSEUM

Your donation helps us conserve and showcase valuable artifacts, artworks, and historical documents for current and future generations to enjoy and learn from. By supporting us, you contribute to the preservation of our collective history and identity.

Our museum serves as a resource for researchers, scholars, and students, providing access to primary sources and curated collections. Your contribution helps us maintain our archives and support scholarly endeavors that advance knowledge and understanding.

By donating to Skedsmo Museum, you leave a lasting impact on our institution and the broader community. Your generosity helps secure our museum's future and ensures that our cultural heritage remains accessible and relevant for years to come.
